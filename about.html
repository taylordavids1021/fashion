who are we

N A T E B looks to the future of children’s clothing to anticipate the needs of our clients by offering a mix of high-end European labels like Gucci, Fendi and Dolce & Gabbana as well as artisanal up-and-coming labels. With over 100+ luxury brands to choose from it is source of inspiration and fun for our global customer base.

With more than 70 years of experience and two shops in Vienna, we are one of Europe’s largest brick and mortar luxury children’s fashion retailers with an ever expanding online shop for kids aged 0-16. Featured in Der Standard and Vogue Bambini, we continue to inspire and surprise the children’s fashion industry.

We offer you online the finest collection of children’s clothing for all occasions. For boys we have vast variety of T-shirts and Polos in cool and vibrant prints, cartoon, graphic images and strips which can be paired with shorts, jeans, and track pants. The fabric is soft and of a good quality, the few top brands available for these are Gini & Jony, Kidsville, Pepe Jeans and United Color of Benetton.

what we do

From fashion delivered easily and efficiently to your door to the award-winning content we create on The Way of Us, Superbalist is thinking big and innovating every day. Fashion is one of the truest ways to express one’s identity, and we believe in giving people the means to do just that.

Our vision

Our vision is to be the most loved and influential fashion destination in South Africa. This motivates us to create fantastic products and customer experiences for our community of shoppers. Our intention is to give our customers the confidence to be who they want to be through everything we do.
